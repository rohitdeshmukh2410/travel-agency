Himalayan Adventure

Itinerary:-

  • Day 1: Arrival in Delhi, evening at leisure.
  • Day 2: Drive to Shimla, evening at leisure.
  • Day 3: Full-day tour of Shimla, visiting Jakhu Temple and Mall Road.
  • Day 4: Drive to Manali, en route visit Kullu Valley.
  • Day 5: Full-day tour of Manali, visiting Hadimba Temple and Vashisht Hot Springs.
  • Day 6: Drive to Dharamshala, evening at leisure.
  • Day 7: Full-day tour of Dharamshala, visiting Dalai Lama Temple and Bhagsu Waterfall.
  • Day 8: Drive to Rishikesh, evening at leisure.
  • Day 9: Full-day tour of Rishikesh, visiting Laxman Jhula and Beatles Ashram.
  • Day 10: River rafting on the Ganges.
  • Day 11: Drive to Haridwar, visit Har Ki Pauri and attend Ganga Aarti.
  • Day 12: Departure from Haridwar.

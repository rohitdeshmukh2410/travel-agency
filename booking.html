    • Email: Send us an email at bookings@yourtravelagency.com with your chosen tour, travel dates, and any special requests.
    • Online Form: Fill out the online booking form on our website.
  3. Payment Details:
    • Deposit: A deposit of 25% of the total tour cost is required to secure your booking.
    • Balance Payment: The remaining balance must be paid at least 30 days before the departure date.
    • Payment Methods: We accept payments via credit card, debit card, bank transfer, and PayPal.
  4. Cancellation Policy:
    • More than 30 days before departure: Full refund minus a ₹2,000 processing fee.
    • 15-30 days before departure: 50% refund.
    • Less than 15 days before departure: No refund.
  5. Travel Insurance:
    • We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover any unforeseen circumstances such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or loss of personal belongings.

Kerala Backwaters and Beaches

Itinerary:-

  • Day 1: Arrival in Kochi, evening at leisure.
  • Day 2: Full-day tour of Kochi, visiting Fort Kochi, Mattancherry Palace, and Chinese Fishing Nets.
  • Day 3: Drive to Munnar, visit tea plantations and Eravikulam National Park.
  • Day 4: Drive to Thekkady, visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Day 5: Drive to Kumarakom, enjoy a houseboat cruise on the backwaters.
  • Day 6: Drive to Kovalam, evening at leisure on the beach.
  • Day 7: Full-day tour of Trivandrum, visiting Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Napier Museum.
  • Day 8: Departure from Trivandrum.
